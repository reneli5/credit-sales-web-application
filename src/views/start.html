If you work for a Local Planning Authority you can approve the sale of Biodiversity Net Gain Statutory Credits for developers - when they need them to make a mandatory 10% biodiversity net gain, as part of their planning application.

You will need:

  • The developer Biodiversity Metric 3.0 calculations

Before you messageProcessor

Please note, credits are only to be used as kast resort, when developers can not meet a mandatory 10% biodiversity net gain - either on their development site or with local offsite units.
